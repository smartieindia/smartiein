FAQ

High-Speed Doors — common questions

How fast do the doors operate?

Opening speeds of 0.8–2.0 m/s depending on size and drive, with radar, loop, pull-cord or interlock activation.

What happens in a power cut?

All doors include manual operation; UPS backup and battery kits are available for critical hygiene openings.
