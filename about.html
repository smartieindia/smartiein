Who We Are

Engineering-first, service-always

Smart India Enterprises (SMARTIE) designs, fabricates, automates and services entrance systems from our Goregaon West, Mumbai base. We are part of the MK family — India's oldest name in entrance automation, established 1980 — and we back every installation with genuine spares, trained technicians and AMC support.

Our in-house SIE motor ecosystem (swing, sliding, shutter, sectional and high-speed drives) means one accountable partner from survey to service. Our transparent polycarbonate folding door is India's only 100% Indian-make system.

Leadership

The people behind SMARTIE

Mohsin Mubin Khan

2nd Generation Entrepreneur · Founder, Smart India Enterprises
Creator of MeasurePro & GatePro · Entrance Automation Specialist · AI-Driven Access Infrastructure

Mohsin Mubin Khan represents the second generation of the MK family’s engineering legacy, combining foundational technical expertise with modern digital innovation. As the Founder and driving force behind Mumbai-based Smart India Enterprises, he has established the company as a premier provider of heavy-duty physical security, custom entrance automation, and intelligent site-management tools.

Carrying forward a strong engineering tradition, Mohsin has earned industry recognition for his high-precision manufacturing and specialized development capabilities. Smart India Enterprises’ solutions have been deployed for elite government, defense, and high-security institutions, including Bharat Electronics (BEL), Smiths Detection, and the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC) core technical units.

Under his leadership, Smart India Enterprises serves as a bridge between heavy industrial engineering and 21st-century digital integration — evolving traditional barrier systems into data-ready access hubs.
